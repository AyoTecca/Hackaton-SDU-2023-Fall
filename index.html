Transforming Healthcare Experience

SDU-Januya is a comprehensive application that addresses the common problems faced by medical centers. With features like easy appointment scheduling, real-time queue status updates, electronic medical records access, online consultations, and feedback system, we aim to provide a seamless and convenient healthcare experience for patients.
features

Key Features

Discover the powerful features that make SDU-Januya the ultimate solution for a professional medical center website.

Convenient Appointment Scheduling

View doctors' schedules with convenient timing and easily make appointments online, even for urgent cases.

Real-time Queue Status and Notifications

Stay informed about the current status of the queue and receive notifications about upcoming appointments, schedule changes, and reception status.

Access to Electronic Medical Records

Effortlessly access your electronic medical record, including examination results and doctors' recommendations. Keep track of your medical history by entering your own data.

Virtual Consultations with Doctors

Have virtual consultations with doctors via video link, eliminating the need for physical presence. Sign up for online consultations for your convenience.
